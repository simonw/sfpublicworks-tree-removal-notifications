    Clay Street (Leavenworth to Jones and Taylor to Mason)

    NOTICE OF TREE REPLACEMENT By San Francisco Department of Public Works (SFDPW) And San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency (SFMTA)

    For more information, please contact: SFMTA.com/TempLanes1Cal TellMuni@SFMTA.com

    LOCATION: Clay Street from Leavenworth Street to Jones Street

    Clay Street from Taylor Street to Mason Street

    # OF TREES: One (1) Evergreen Pear (Pyrus Kawakamii)

    One (1) Maidenhair Tree (Ginko Biloba)

    POSTING PERIOD (30 days) From: 04/24/2023 Through: 05/24/2023

    The SFMTA, in partnership with SFDPW, is planning to replace the two (2) trees listed above to install transit lanes approved for these blocks. The trees overhang into the curbside lane, preventing Muni buses and Recology trucks from operating there without obstruction. In addition to replacing the two trees, the SFMTA will strive to plant up to five (5) more trees on or near Clay Street to help support the health of the urban canopy. These mitigations totaling seven (7) new trees are equal to or greater than the value of the two trees. Members of the public objecting to this action must protest in writing to SFMTA. Protests must be made within the posting period or postmarked no later than last date of the posting period. Protests can be mailed or emailed to the following: San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency ATTN: Erin McMillan, 1 S. Van Ness Ave, 3rd Floor, San Francisco, CA 94103 Email: TellMuni@SFMTA.com If the tree removal is protested within the posting period, a public hearing will be scheduled.

    Reason(s) for Removal: Main tree trunks lack required clearance over roadway; Direct conflict with Muni bus and refuse collection truck operations.

    Replacement Tree(s): Yes

    Other: SFMTA to plant seven (7) new trees along or near Clay Street from Larkin Street to Mason Street
