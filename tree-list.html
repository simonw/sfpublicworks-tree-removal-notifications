Protests must be made within the posting period or postmarked no later than last date of the posting period. Protests can be mailed or emailed to the following: San Francisco Municipal Transportation Agency ATTN: Erin McMillan, 1 S. Van Ness Ave, 3rd Floor, San Francisco, CA 94103 Email: TellMuni@SFMTA.com If the tree removal is protested within the posting period, a public hearing will be scheduled.
